# Analysis of support team efficiency
Aim:
The goal of the project was to explore the support team of the company in order to answer the stakeholder's questions (that can be found in .ipynb file) and improve the quality of service.

Structure:
The project was made in colab environment. .ipynb contains code and vizualization of results. The .csv file is the original dataset. During the research matplotlib and pandas were used. To recreate results download csv in google drive and connect your disk to colab, change paths.
